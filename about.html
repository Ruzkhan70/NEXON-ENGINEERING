Who We Are

Nexon Engineering is a trusted provider of industrial repair, maintenance, and automation services. We specialize in machine servicing, industrial electrical work, and custom engineering solutions designed to improve productivity and reliability. With a commitment to precision, innovation, and customer satisfaction, we deliver high-quality engineering services that keep your operations running at their best.

At Nexon Engineering, we believe in quality workmanship, transparent service, and long-term solutions. Whether it is a routine service, a complex breakdown, a new installation, or a complete factory maintenance contract, we are committed to delivering results that exceed expectations.

Driven by innovation and guided by professionalism, we aim to be a trusted partner for industries across Sri Lanka, providing services that enhance operational excellence and support sustainable growth.

Our Mission

To deliver reliable, high-quality engineering solutions in machine repair, maintenance, automation, and industrial electrical services. We strive to innovate through advanced technologies, ensure customer satisfaction with integrity and professionalism, develop a skilled and safety-focused workforce, and build long-term partnerships by providing consistent, value-driven support.

Our Vision

To be Sri Lanka's leading and most trusted engineering services provider, delivering innovative, sustainable, and high-precision industrial solutions that empower businesses to achieve operational excellence.
